APP FEATURE DEVELOPING

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Introduction

Our team was tasked with designing and developing a new feature for an existing app. We analyzed the current user experience, identified improvement opportunities, and created a feature that would seamlessly integrate with the app.

My role:

UX researcher
UI designer
Illustrator
Web designer

Tool:

Figma

Time:

2 weeks

Summary

We worked the new feature for an existing app, aiming to improve the user experience. Through research, wireframing, and prototyping, we focused on making the feature easy to use and a great fit with the app’s design.

RESULT

“Bloom Board” is a pin board where users can go on to leave notes daily and look or reply to others. It allows users to express themselves while spreading positivity and kindness with the community. They make connections by sharing experiences, optimistic mindsets or asking for advice through little notes also by leaving inspirational or relatable replies.

Instruction at the top encourages users to write positive things, there’s also an AI assistant to scan for bad words and negative comments.

RESEARCH

App research

Primary Purpose

Everyday reminder to be kinder to yourself, no matter how busy life gets
Create a safe space for you to take a break from the stresses in real life
Help you practice emotional wellbeing by doing daily self-kindness activities
Spread your kindness with others in the community

Intended users

People who want to relax and relieve stress
People who want to improve mental wellbeing (have a positive view to cope well with the everyday stresses of life)
People who like to do cute things, like decorating, collecting, and taking care of plants

Audience Key Insights:

Goals and Motivations

Through reflecting on their lives and planning for the future, people could feel more relaxed

Everyone does different activities to relax, and feel comfortable while trying to forget the stress in short term

Struggles

Most users find its hard to relax and their recent solution could not help them feel better

Habits and activities

Everyone does different activities to relax, and feel comfortable while trying to forget the stress in short term

Barrier

People believe that they can overcome the stress themself, they find other activities is more helpful than playing the game.

Problem & solution

Problem statements

Users find the game unappealing because it feels less useful than other activities and fails to help them relax or relieve stress. Some users find it hard to find the tasks they have to do and would feel frustrated, discouraging them from continuing. Many also feel guilty about spending time on games or relaxation.

How might we

How might we design A Kinder World to effectively guide users in exploring tasks that promote long-term engagement while helping them relax, reflect on their emotions, and increase mental health awareness without feeling guilty about prioritizing self-care?

what I have learned?

I learned that understanding the audience is the most important step before diving into development. It’s essential to ensure the feature meets their needs. One thing I hadn't initially considered was the level of simplicity and clarity needed for a seamless user experience.

Another key takeaway from this project is that balancing user needs with business goals is just as important as understanding the audience. Defining the problem clearly and sticking to it acts like a navigation system—it keeps our ideas focused, prevents us from getting lost in the design process, and helps us brainstorm more efficiently.
